Agent Based Model 1

I have developed a Agent based model in which Agents interact with a environment. Functions include: - Move around the environment in a random way - eating the environment and throwing up if they have eaten too much - sharing their resources with with neighbours, or if they have not eaten enough stealing all their neighbours resorces. - 'Wolfs' walk around the environment and identify if they would eat the agents - Display the output animated on a GUI More information is available on the Readme.txt file.
